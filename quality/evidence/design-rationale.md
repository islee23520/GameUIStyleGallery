# Design Rationale Evidence

Design rationale records why a quality claim is admissible.

## Evidence Structures

- issue, position, and argument;
- question, option, and criterion;
- claim, ground, and warrant;
- goal, strategy, context, assumption, and evidence.

## Can Support

- a design decision considered alternatives;
- a claim has a warrant;
- a limitation or counterclaim is visible;
- design debt is intentional rather than accidental.

## Cannot Prove

- the selected option is empirically best;
- a user-facing outcome without user or task evidence;
- accessibility, usability, or visual quality by itself.
